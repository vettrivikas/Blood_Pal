About

This is a non-profitable organization, whose main goal is to help the people who are in desperate need of blood. What we do is, basically we act as a bridge that connects two points. i.e., we connect the blood donor and a nearest blood camp in the donor’s location. The organiser of the blood camp will update their camp details in the website, and we will deliver the information to the blood donor who have signed up with us, if he/she is interested in donating blood they could enroll in the app about their donation and their information will be sent to the organiser.Nosis is a non-profitable organization, whose main goal is to help the people who are in desperate need of blood.
